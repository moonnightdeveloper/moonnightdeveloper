Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive support responses?

We typically respond to all support requests within 24 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please use our live chat feature for immediate assistance.

Do you offer custom website development services?

Yes, we do offer custom development services. While we primarily focus on creating templates, we're available for custom projects. Please contact us with your project requirements for a quote.

Can I modify the templates after purchase?

Absolutely! All our templates come with full source code and are completely customizable. You're free to modify them to fit your specific needs and branding requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for larger custom projects.

Do you offer refunds for template purchases?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally don't offer refunds once a template has been downloaded. However, if you encounter technical issues that prevent you from using the template, we'll work with you to resolve them or provide a refund if we cannot fix the issue.
